# CampusPulse Design Brief

## CDI Name

CampusPulse

## Short Description

CampusPulse is a student-powered campus issue tracker for NIT Trichy. Students report everyday problems, vote on what matters most, and follow visible progress from report to resolution.

## Problem

Campus problems often travel through WhatsApp groups, corridor conversations, or one-off complaints. Important issues lose visibility because there is no single student-friendly place to collect, prioritize, and track them.

## Visual Direction

- Mood: urgent, optimistic, civic, student-led.
- Core metaphor: campus signals becoming visible pulses.
- Palette: warm paper base, deep ink, teal, coral, lime, and gold accents.
- Typography: bold headlines with compact supporting copy.
- Layout system: strong blocks, signal rings, status chips, and tracker cards.
